Empowering Individuals with Disabilities in Tanzania's Lake Zone

Kuwawezesha Watu Wenye Ulemavu Katika Ukanda wa Ziwa Tanzania

CAVITRD is dedicated to enhancing the lives of children and adults living with disabilities across Tanzania's Lake Zone region. We strive to create sustainable impact through comprehensive support systems and community integration.

CAVITRD imejikita katika kuboresha maisha ya watoto na watu wazima wenye ulemavu katika eneo la Ukanda wa Ziwa Tanzania. Tunajitahidi kuunda athari endelevu kupitia mifumo ya usaidizi kamili na ujumuishaji wa jamii.

Our Story & Mission

Historia Yetu na Misheni

CAVITRD is a registered Tanzanian NGO with Registration number 14NGO/R/4896, dealing with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairment, and trauma-related disabilities.

CAVITRD ni ASHI iliyosajiliwa nchini Tanzania na nambari ya usajili 14NGO/R/4896, inayoshughulikia watoto na watu wazima walio na kasoro za kuzaliwa, upofu, na ulemavu unaohusiana na majeraha.

We work with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairments, and trauma-related disabilities. Our efforts focus on rural communities, especially across the Lake Zone region of Tanzania.

Tunafanya kazi na watoto na watu wazima walio na kasoro za kuzaliwa, upofu, na ulemavu unaohusiana na majeraha. Juhudi zetu zinalenga jamii za vijijini, hasa katika eneo la Ukanda wa Ziwa Tanzania.

What We Do

Tunachofanya

Awareness Campaigns

Kampeni za Sensitization

We conduct outreach and community workshops to reduce stigma and raise awareness on disability inclusion.

Tunafanya ukumbi wa jamii na mafunzo ili kupunguza chuki na kuongeza ufahamu juu ya ujumuishaji wa ulemavu.

Education Community

Assistive Devices

Vifaa vya Kusaidia

We distribute wheelchairs, walkers, crutches, and other devices to children and adults in need.

Tunasambaza viti vya magurudumu, vifaa vya kutembelea, mikongojo, na vifaa vingine kwa watoto na watu wazima wenye uhitaji.

Equipment Support

Medical Support

Usaidizi wa Kimatibabu

We connect families with rehabilitation, physiotherapy, surgery, and clinical referrals for disability care.

Tunaunganisha familia na upatikanaji wa tiba ya urekebishaji, fizikia, upasuaji, na rufaa za kliniki kwa utunzaji wa ulemavu.

Healthcare Rehabilitation

Caregiver Training

Mafunzo ya Walezi

We empower parents, caregivers, and health workers with training on inclusive care and home-based therapy.

Tunawawezesha wazazi, walezi, na wafanyikazi wa afya kwa mafunzo juu ya utunzaji jumuishi na tiba ya nyumbani.

Training Empowerment

Advocacy & Inclusion

Utetezi na Ujumuishaji

We work with leaders and institutions to push for inclusive policies in education, health, and community planning.

Tunafanya kazi na viongozi na taasisi kusukuma sera za ujumuishaji katika elimu, afya, na upangaji wa jamii.

Advocacy Policy

Community Support

Usaidizi wa Jamii

Building sustainable community support systems for long-term disability inclusion and support.

Kujenga mifumo endelevu ya usaidizi wa jamii kwa ujumuishaji wa muda mrefu wa ulemavu na usaidizi.

Inclusive Outreach for Children with Disabilities

Ufikiaji Jumuishi kwa Watoto Wenye Ulemavu

In May, our team reached 20+ children in Ilemela District living with mobility-related disabilities. We distributed 4 walkers, 2 crutches, and 2 wheelchairs.

Mwezi Mei, timu yetu ilifika watoto 20+ katika Wilaya ya Ilemela wanaoishi na ulemavu unaohusiana na uwezo wa kutembea. Tulisambaza vifaa 4 vya kutembelea, mikongojo 2, na viti 2 vya magurudumu.

New Partnership for Medical Referrals

CAVITRD ilishirikiana na Kituo cha Afya cha Mwanza kutoa rufaa za upasuaji na fizikia kwa watoto wenye uhitaji.
