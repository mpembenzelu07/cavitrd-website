Our Vision & Mission

The guiding principles that shape everything we do

Our Vision

Provision of surgical treatment to children born with congenital anomalies and those who acquire trauma-related disabilities. We also facilitate necessary education to communities susceptible to certain congenital anomalies and support prevention by decreasing the incidence of trauma-related disabilities.

Our Mission

Provision of quality health care services and education to rural areas through organized surgical camps and outreach programs in the regions.

Our Objectives

Enhancing society's awareness of available treatment options and modalities for various congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities. Advocating for prevention and protection measures to reduce the development of anomalies and trauma-related disabilities.

Our Core Values

The foundation of our organization's culture and work

Dignity

We respect every individual's humanity and inherent worth in all our interactions.

Equity

We believe in fair access to care and opportunities for all, regardless of background.

Transparency

We act with honesty, openness, and accountability in all our operations.

Compassion

We lead with empathy, kindness, and genuine care for every person we serve.
