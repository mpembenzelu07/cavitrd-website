Empowering Individuals with Disabilities in Tanzania's Lake Zone

Join Our Mission

Our Mission

CAVITRD is dedicated to enhancing the lives of children and adults living with disabilities across Tanzania's Lake Zone region. We strive to create sustainable impact through comprehensive support systems and community integration.

Our Work

We provide essential services including surgical interventions, mobility assistance, and inclusive educational support for individuals with congenital or trauma-related disabilities. Our collaborative approach involves partnerships with local clinics, caregivers, and community leaders to reduce stigma and improve access to quality care.
