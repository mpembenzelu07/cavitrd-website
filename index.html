Our Mission

To promote inclusive and equitable support systems for children with disabilities in underserved rural areas through education, advocacy, healthcare access, and community engagement.

What We Do

Awareness Campaigns

Workshops and outreach to reduce stigma and increase community inclusion.

Assistive Devices

Providing walkers, wheelchairs, and training for children and caregivers.

Medical Access

Helping families reach physiotherapy, surgical referrals, and health education.
