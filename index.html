What We Do

We provide access to surgical treatment, mobility aids, and inclusive education support for children with congenital or trauma-related disabilities. Our team works across the Lake Zone region of Tanzania, building partnerships with clinics, caregivers, and local leaders to reduce stigma and improve access to care.
