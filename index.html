About CAVITRD

CAVITRD is a Tanzanian NGO with Registration number 14NGO/R/4896, dealing with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairment, and trauma-related disabilities.

Our Goals

To improve access to assistive devices, medical care, education, and community-based awareness in underserved rural areas.
