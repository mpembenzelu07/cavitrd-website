About Us

CAVITRD stands for Centre for Advocacy and Visibility of Inclusive Transformation in Rural Disabilities. We are a registered non-governmental organization in Tanzania under Reg. No. 14NGO/R/4896.

We are committed to supporting children and adults living with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairments, and trauma-related disabilities across the Lake Zone region of Tanzania. Our work is grounded in inclusion, empowerment, and dignity for all.

Our Mission

To promote inclusive and equitable support systems for people with disabilities in rural areas through education, advocacy, healthcare access, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A society where every child and adult with disabilities is visible, valued, and fully included in all aspects of life.

Our Objectives

  • Support early detection and intervention for children with disabilities.
  • Provide access to assistive devices and essential medical care.
  • Raise awareness in communities and reduce stigma.
  • Build caregiver capacity through training and resources.
  • Advocate for inclusive public policies and programs.

Our Core Values

  • Inclusion: Every voice matters.
  • Empowerment: We uplift children, families, and communities.
  • Respect: We value dignity, empathy, and understanding.
  • Accountability: We operate with transparency and integrity.

Our History

CAVITRD began in Mwanza through grassroots efforts by a team of passionate individuals: caregivers, health workers, teachers, and community leaders. They noticed the gap in services for children living with disabilities in rural areas.
