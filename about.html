Our Story

Who We Are

CAVITRD is a registered Tanzanian NGO with Registration number 14NGO/R/4896, dealing with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairment, and trauma-related disabilities.

We work with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairments, and trauma-related disabilities. Our efforts focus on rural communities, especially across the Lake Zone region of Tanzania.

Through organized surgical camps, community education, and comprehensive support services, we strive to create lasting positive change in the lives of those we serve.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision & Mission

The guiding principles that drive our work and define our purpose

Our Vision

Provision of surgical treatment to children born with congenital anomalies and those who acquire trauma-related disabilities. We also facilitate necessary education to communities susceptible to certain congenital anomalies and support prevention by decreasing the incidence of trauma-related disabilities.

Our Mission

Provision of quality health care services and education to rural areas through organized surgical camps and outreach programs in the regions.

Our Objectives

The specific goals we work towards achieving every day

01

Enhancing Awareness

Enhancing society's awareness of available treatment options and modalities for various congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities. We believe that informed communities are empowered communities.

02

Advocating Prevention

Advocating for prevention and protection measures to reduce the development of anomalies and trauma-related disabilities. Our prevention programs focus on education, early intervention, and community engagement.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every action we take

Dignity

We respect every individual's humanity and inherent worth, treating all with the respect they deserve as human beings.

Equity

We believe in fair access to care and opportunities for all, regardless of background, disability, or circumstances.

We act with honesty, openness, and accountability in all our operations, building trust with communities and partners.

Our History

The journey of CAVITRD from humble beginnings to making a lasting impact

CAVITRD began in Mwanza, where a group of caregivers, community leaders, and health workers saw how children with disabilities were being left behind. From a few small meetings and outreach sessions, we grew into a recognized organization.

2017

Foundation

CAVITRD was founded by a group of dedicated healthcare professionals and community leaders in Mwanza who recognized the urgent need for specialized care for children and adults with disabilities.

2018

Registration

Officially registered as a Tanzanian NGO with Registration number 14NGO/R/4896, marking the beginning of our formal operations and expanded reach.

2019

First Surgical Camps

Conducted our first surgical outreach camps in remote Lake Zone communities, providing life-changing procedures to those who had no access to specialized healthcare.

2023

Expanded Reach

Our story continues with every village we reach, every family we support, and every child we help thrive. We have now impacted thousands of lives across the Lake Zone region.
