About CAVITRD

CAVITRD a registered Tanzanian NGO with Registration number 14NGO/R/4896, dealing with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairment, and trauma-related disabilities.

We work with children and adults with congenital or acquired anomalies, vision impairments, and trauma-related disabilities. Our efforts focus on rural communities, especially across the Lake Zone region of Tanzania.

Our Mission

To build inclusive and accessible systems of care for individuals with disabilities in underserved areas through education, healthcare, and advocacy.

Our Vision

Provision of surgical treatment to children born with congenital anomalies and those who acquire trauma-related disabilities. We also facilitate necessary education to communities susceptible to certain congenital anomalies and support prevention by decreasing the incidence of trauma-related disabilities.

Our Mission

Provision of quality health care services and education to rural areas through organized surgical camps and outreach programs in the regions.

Our Objectives

  • Enhancing society's awareness of available treatment options and modalities for various congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities.
  • Advocating for prevention and protection measures to reduce the development of anomalies and trauma-related disabilities.

Our History

CAVITRD began in Mwanza, where a group of caregivers, community leaders, and health workers saw how children with disabilities were being left behind. From a few small meetings and outreach sessions, we grew into a recognized organization. Our story continues with every village we reach, every family we support, and every child we help thrive.
