Record-Breaking Surgical Camp Transforms 150 Lives

October 15, 2023 Mwanza Region

Our largest surgical outreach camp to date successfully provided life-changing procedures to 150 children and adults with congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities. The week-long camp brought together 25 medical professionals and served communities across three districts.
