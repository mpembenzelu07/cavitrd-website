What We Do

1. Outreach to Children with Disabilities

We visit families in rural areas and identify children in need of mobility aids, therapy, and support. Our goal is to reduce stigma and improve lives.

2. Medical Referrals

We partner with health centers to connect children with doctors, physiotherapists, and surgeons. We assist in arranging transportation and follow-ups.

3. Awareness & Community Education

We conduct training for caregivers, schools, and local leaders on disability inclusion, prevention, and early detection of congenital conditions.

4. Assistive Devices & Support

We provide donated or locally made mobility aids like crutches, walkers, and wheelchairs to children who need them.
