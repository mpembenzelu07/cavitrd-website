What We Do

Comprehensive programs and services transforming lives across Tanzania's Lake Zone region. Our work focuses on surgical care, vision support, and community education.

Our Core Programs

Integrated services addressing the complete needs of children and adults with disabilities

Surgical Outreach Camps

We organize mobile surgical camps in remote areas to provide life-changing procedures for children and adults with congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities.

  • Free surgical procedures for congenital anomalies
  • Trauma-related disability treatment
  • Post-operative care and follow-up

Vision Impairment Support

Comprehensive services for individuals with vision impairments, including screening, treatment, and assistive devices to improve quality of life.

  • Vision screening and diagnosis
  • Provision of assistive devices

Surgical Outreach Program

Our surgical camps provide essential medical interventions in remote communities where access to specialized healthcare is limited. We bring together teams of skilled surgeons, nurses, and medical professionals to perform life-changing procedures.

These camps focus on congenital anomalies such as cleft lip/palate, clubfoot, and other birth defects, as well as trauma-related disabilities requiring surgical intervention.
