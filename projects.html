What We Do

Disability Awareness Campaigns

We organize community-based awareness sessions to reduce stigma, educate caregivers, and promote early identification of congenital and acquired disabilities.

Assistive Device Distribution

CAVITRD distributes essential assistive devices such as walkers, crutches, and wheelchairs to children and adults living with disabilities in underserved areas.

Medical Referral & Rehabilitation

We connect children with medical specialists for surgeries, physical therapy, and follow-up care in partnership with local clinics and hospitals.

Caregiver Training & Support

We empower parents and guardians through practical training in rehabilitation techniques and emotional support for caring for children with special needs.

Advocacy & Policy Engagement

CAVITRD advocates for inclusive health and education policies by working with village leaders, schools, and government bodies across the Lake Zone region.
