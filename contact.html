Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about CAVITRD

How can I make a donation to CAVITRD?

You can make a donation through bank transfer, mobile money, or by visiting our office. All donations are tax-deductible and you will receive a receipt for your contribution. For detailed donation information, please contact our finance department at donations@cavitrd.org.

What types of volunteer opportunities are available?

We offer various volunteer opportunities including medical professionals for surgical camps, community educators, administrative support, and field coordinators. Volunteer requirements vary by position. Please contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@cavitrd.org for current opportunities.

How can my organization partner with CAVITRD?

We welcome partnerships with healthcare organizations, educational institutions, corporations, and other NGOs. Partnership opportunities include joint programs, resource sharing, capacity building, and advocacy initiatives. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@cavitrd.org to discuss collaboration.

How can someone access your surgical services?

Individuals can access our surgical services through our community outreach programs, referrals from local health centers, or by contacting our medical team directly. Services are provided free of charge to eligible patients. Contact our medical coordinator at medical@cavitrd.org for more information.
