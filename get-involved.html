Get Involved with CAVITRD

You can play a vital role in helping children with disabilities in rural Tanzania. Whether you volunteer, donate supplies, partner with us, or spread awareness — your support makes a difference.

👩‍⚕️ Volunteer Your Skills

Are you a health worker, physiotherapist, or educator? Join us in community outreach and training.

🎒 Donate Supplies

Help children by donating assistive devices, hygiene items, school materials, or mobility equipment.

🤝 Become a Partner

We collaborate with clinics, schools, and NGOs. Let’s work together to support inclusive transformation.

📢 Spread Awareness

Follow and share our mission on social media. Help us make disabilities more visible in rural communities.
